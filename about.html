About Us

Founded in 2012, UNXSYS is a collaborative mentoring program modeled around an actual tech start-up. Students don't just learn how to program, they learn how to collaborate with others and the tools needed to do so. Emphasis is placed on the variety of career paths within the tech industry, and how these diverse roles interact with each other to make a functioning team. Working with a tech professional, mentees work on open source projects and even as consultants for non-profits and local businesses.

Besides helping our students start their careers in the tech industry, our primary goal is continuing education and growth for ourselves, our team, and our peers. We seek to turn all of our students into future teachers. Our belief is that in doing so they become effective leaders that can clearly explain their knowledge, enabling them to give the most back to the community.
