Not sure where to get started? We can help.

Development

Our combined decades of experience in web development has helped dozens of local businesses and nonprofits create modern, responsive websites using several different languages, frameworks, and CMS over the years, including but not limited to: Flask, Django, Bootstrap, Skeleton, Foundation, Joomla, Sitefinity, and Drupal.

We have also explored some of the exciting possibilities provided through isomorphic application development and generating Single Page Applications using JavaScript, node.js, and UI frameworks like React and Vue. Depending on student interest we've also explored other niche development verticals like eCommerce and higher education.

Systems

Though Linux is our primary focus, we've done work on pretty much any environment you can name: in the cloud or bare metal, from commerical UNIX to BSD, and the occasional Windows.

As a result of our variety of backgrounds we provide a thorough overview of the various types of systems students will encounter in the professional sphere. Depending on their professional goals students can practice and get comfortable working on server migrations, planning, building, and scaling infrastructure, and many other tasks required in DevOps and systems administration.

Mentoring

In addition to development and systems administration, our mentoring program encourages students to practice teaching what they've learned to peers through pair programming, rubber duck debugging, and project development. As students build their portfolios and prepare for the job search, we assist them in resume writing, interview practice, and whiteboarding challenges.

Finally, since the rapidly changing nature of this industry requires staying up-to-date on emerging trends, so we encourage students to research and give short presententations on higher-level topics of their choosing.
