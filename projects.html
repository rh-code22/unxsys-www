Projects

With our diverse experience and expertise in the industry, we've worked on a variety of projects for clients with very different needs. Below are just a few examples of the projects on which we have collaborated. This isn't just a profession - it's really our passion.

Browser Extensions

As frequent users of Chrome, developing browser extensions have proven to be an excellent means of practicing development skills and as an additional avenue to understand all aspects of the web.

Concierge

Concierge is UNXSYS's dynamic deployment system based on iPXE. Initial deployment and configuration of bare-metal and virtual systems is handled through a web-based system that can even chain-load third-party deployment systems.

Knowledgebase

Over the years, UNXSYS has hosted numerous lecture series and classes on a variety of technical topics. Knowledgebase takes this information and makes them into living documents and syllabi.

Responsive

We've found that while a number of our clients might think they have the website they want, the experience for users varies greatly depending on their client. Along with juggling the peculiarities of Chrome, Firefox, and Internet Explorer, we've helped many clients to have a website that looks just as good when viewed on a mobile platform.

Education and Community

Through our love of all things Linux and Open Source we've tried to give back by training interested students with a series of free workshop, classes, and lectures with low student to teacher ratios - to allow for direct interaction and individual attention between mentors and students.

Class topics have included the following topics and may expand in the future, given demand:

  • Front and Back-end Web Development
  • DevOps and Systems Administration
  • Technical Writing and Documentation
  • Project Management and Agile Methodology
  • Tools and Pipelines for Development and Deployment
  • Security Practices and Debugging
